## Company Overview

### Business Model: The Advertising-Funded Internet

Alphabet operates a unique business model: **provide free services to billions of users, monetize through advertising and cloud services.**

**Core Flywheel:**
1. Build exceptional products (Search, Maps, YouTube, Gmail, Chrome, Android)
2. Attract billions of users (8 billion globally)
3. Collect usage data to improve products and target ads
4. Sell advertising to businesses who want to reach those users
5. Reinvest profits into better products → repeat

### Revenue Composition (2024: $350B)

| Segment | Revenue | % of Total | YoY Growth | Description |
|---------|---------|------------|------------|-------------|
| **Google Search & Other** | $200B | 57% | +10% | Search ads, Google properties |
| **YouTube Ads** | $35B | 10% | +12% | Video advertising |
| **Google Cloud** | $38B | 11% | +30% | GCP, Workspace, infrastructure |
| **Google Network** | $35B | 10% | +5% | AdSense, AdMob partner revenue |
| **Other Bets & Services** | $42B | 12% | +15% | Play Store, Devices, Pixel, subscriptions |

**Key Observations:**
- Search still dominant (57%) but Cloud growing fastest (30%+ YoY)
- YouTube undermonetized vs potential (Netflix: $30B revenue with 250M subs)
- Network declining as partners build direct relationships
- Other Bets includes $1B+ Waymo revenue

### Organizational Structure (Post-2023 Reorganization)

**Alphabet Inc.** (Parent holding company)
- **Google LLC** (Core business)
  - Search & Advertising
  - Google Cloud (CEO: Thomas Kurian)
  - YouTube (CEO: Neal Mohan)
  - Google DeepMind (CEO: Demis Hassabis) - *Merged Brain + DeepMind in April 2023*
  - Platforms & Devices (Pixel, Nest, Fitbit)
- **Other Bets**
  - Waymo (Self-driving)
  - Verily (Life sciences)
  - Wing (Delivery drones)
  - X (Moonshot factory)

**Key Leadership:**
- Sundar Pichai: CEO of Alphabet & Google
- Demis Hassabis: CEO of Google DeepMind (merged AI division)
- Jeff Dean: Chief Scientist (elevated role overseeing all AI research)
- Thomas Kurian: CEO of Google Cloud (joined 2018 from Oracle)
- Ruth Porat: President & Chief Investment Officer
- Philipp Schindler: Chief Business Officer (advertising)

### Historical Performance (2015-2024)

**Revenue Growth:**
- 2015: $74.5B
- 2020: $182.5B (2.45x in 5 years)
- 2024: $350.0B (4.7x in 9 years)
- **CAGR: 18.4%**

**Profitability Evolution:**
- Gross Margin: 62% (2015) → 56% (2024) - *Mix shift to Cloud lowers blended margin*
- Operating Margin: 24% (2015) → 30% (2024) - *Operating leverage despite Cloud drag*
- Net Margin: 22% (2015) → 28% (2024)
- ROE: 14% (2015) → 35.5% (2024) - *Exceptional improvement*

**Key Milestones:**
- 2015: Restructured as Alphabet (holding company for Google + Other Bets)
- 2017: Google Cloud becomes reportable segment ($4B revenue)
- 2019: First Cloud revenue disclosure, showing massive losses
- 2022: ChatGPT launch (November) triggers "Code Red"
- 2023: DeepMind + Brain merger, Gemini announced
- 2024: Cloud first annual profit, dividend initiated, DOJ monopoly ruling
