## Quantitative Metrics & KPIs

### Financial KPIs (Investment Decision Metrics)

**Must-Track Metrics (Quarterly):**

| Metric | Q4 2024 | Q1 2025 Target | Q4 2025 Target | 2027 Target | Significance |
|--------|---------|----------------|----------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | +14% YoY | >12% | >13% | >14% | Core growth |
| **Operating Margin** | 30% | >29% | >30% | >31% | Profitability |
| **Cloud Revenue** | $50B run rate | $60B | $70B | $110B | Growth driver |
| **Cloud Op. Margin** | 13% | >15% | >18% | >25% | Profitability inflection |
| **Search Market Share** | 89.6% | >88% | >87% | >85% | Defensive metric |
| **Free Cash Flow** | $62.5B | $40B+ | $50B+ | $110B | Capital return capacity |
| **ROE** | 35.5% | >33% | >34% | >36% | Capital efficiency |

**Interpretation:**

- Revenue growth <10% = warning sign (secular decline starting)
- Cloud margin <15% by Q4 2025 = commoditization concern
- Search share <85% = crisis (accelerating disruption)
- FCF < $40B in any quarter = CapEx overhang concern

### Red Flags (Sell Signals)

**Immediate Sell Triggers:**

1. **Search share <80%** - Structural disruption confirmed
2. **Revenue growth <5% for 2 consecutive quarters** - Ex-COVID, this is crisis
3. **Cloud operating margin negative** - Regression after profitability
4. **CFO/CEO departure** - Leadership instability
5. **Material cybersecurity breach** - Brand/trust damage

**Warning Signals (Reduce Position):**

1. Search share 82-85% (monitoring, not crisis yet)
2. Cloud growth <20% (secular slowdown)
3. AI model benchmarks falling behind (Gemini 3.0 worse than GPT-5)
4. Multiple quarters of FCF <$40B (CapEx overhang)
5. Regulatory remedies worse than expected (Chrome divestiture ordered)

### Green Flags (Add to Position)

**Buy More Signals:**

1. **Search revenue growing** despite AI disruption - Thesis validated
2. **Cloud margins >20%** - AWS-level profitability reached
3. **Gemini wins blind tests** vs GPT/Claude - Technical leadership
4. **Waymo profitability** - Optionality paying off
5. **Stock <$250** - Valuation too cheap
