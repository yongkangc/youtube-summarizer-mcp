# YouTube Video Summary Prompt

Analyze this YouTube video transcript and provide a clear, concise summary with the following structure:

## Summary
Brief 2-3 sentence overview of the video's main topic and purpose.

## Key Insights
- List the most important points, ideas, or takeaways (3-7 bullet points)
- Focus on actionable insights and core concepts
- Be specific and concrete

## Notable Quotes or Highlights
- Include any particularly impactful statements or examples (if applicable)

Keep the entire response concise and avoid unnecessary verbosity. Focus on what matters most.
