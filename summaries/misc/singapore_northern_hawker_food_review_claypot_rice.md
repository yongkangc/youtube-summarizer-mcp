- Located at Delta Road with additional outlets (including Bishan area)
- **Signature Claypot Rice**: Cooked over charcoal for 20 minutes, then angled to control fire for even cooking
- Ingredients include chicken thigh, salted fish, lap cheong (Chinese sausage)

- The "guo ba" (crispy rice at the bottom) is described as "like a snack, like rice crispy" - not burnt/cancerous tasting
- **Braised Duck with Yam**: Pan-fried first for crispy skin, then steamed for a long period
- The yam infuses into the soup creating a thick, luxurious broth
- Duck is described as "tender on another level" - silky, with no gaminess
- An ex-defense minister was spotted eating there as a regular customer
