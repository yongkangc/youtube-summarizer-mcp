# Lossdog launch promotion

[YouTube source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Njys_w8P9-k)

## Summary

This is a **96-second Lossdog advertisement**, not finance education.

Tom Sosnoff promotes Lossdog as an AI platform for estimating professional market value, potential net worth, and portfolio optimization. He says it was trained on millions of résumés and hundreds of millions of data points, and claims user uploads create a reinforcing data moat.

Launch offer:

- total **$1M cryptocurrency** distributed across 50,000 starter positions;
- assets named: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Stellar, and Solana;
- free first-year Lossdog subscription;
- limited to first **50,000** users;
- résumé/portfolio upload required;
- speaker says no credit card required.

## Caveats

- Crypto amount and allocation per user are not specified in video.
- Accuracy, methodology, data provenance, and performance of salary/net-worth forecasts are not demonstrated.
- “Data moat” is company marketing claim, not independently verified.
- Uploading résumé and portfolio involves sensitive personal and financial data; privacy, retention, model-training consent, deletion rights, and security terms should be reviewed first.
- Scarcity language is marketing urgency, not investment edge.

## One-line summary

Lossdog offers free subscription plus small crypto starter position in exchange for early adoption and valuable user data.
