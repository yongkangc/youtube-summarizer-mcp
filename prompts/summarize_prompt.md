# YouTube Video Summary Prompt

Analyze this YouTube video transcript and provide a comprehensive, detailed summary with the following structure:

## Summary
A thorough 3-5 sentence overview of the video's main topic, purpose, and context. Provide enough detail to understand what the video is about and why it matters.

## Key Insights
- List ALL important points, ideas, arguments, and takeaways from the video
- Include as many bullet points as needed to capture the full scope of content
- Organize by themes or topics when appropriate
- Focus on actionable insights, core concepts, and significant ideas
- Be specific and concrete with examples where relevant
- Include supporting details and context for each point

## Main Arguments or Thesis
- What are the primary claims or positions being presented?
- What evidence or reasoning supports these claims?
- Are there any counterarguments or alternative perspectives discussed?

## Notable Quotes or Highlights
- Include particularly impactful statements, memorable phrases, or key examples
- Provide context for each quote
- Include speaker attribution if multiple speakers

## Practical Takeaways
- What can viewers apply or learn from this video?
- Are there specific recommendations, steps, or advice given?

## Additional Context
- Any relevant background information, references, or related topics mentioned
- Important caveats, limitations, or nuances discussed

Prioritize completeness and accuracy over brevity. The goal is to provide a detailed picture of the video's content so someone can understand the full scope without watching it.
